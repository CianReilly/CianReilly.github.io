This project aims to recreate the Detroit techno sound using software and hardware instruments. The research side of the project looked at:

1. The History of Detroit Techno.

2. The Technologies the Belleville Three used.

3. The Influence the Belleville Three had on Dance Music.

4. Two interviews with electronic music producers.

Then the remainder of the report aimed to detail the process of creating two compositions, as well as the findings and conclusions gained from the completion of the project. The aim for the practical part was to create two tracks, one using hardware instruments and one using software instruments, and try to re-create the sounds of early Detroit techno on both tracks.
