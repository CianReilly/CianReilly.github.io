This project attempts to recreate the sounds of early Detroit techno using both hardware and software instruments. The research section of this project investigates the history and technologies of a group of producers from a town called Belleville, just outside Detroit, named The Belleville Three and how they created the sound of Detroit techno. It then examines the influence they have had on electronic music and other genres besides as well as two interviews with electronic music producers to get some insights into music production.

The software composition was composed using Logic Pro and samples from hardware instruments that were used to create early Detroit techno. The hardware composition was recorded using a Roland TR-606 Drum Machine, an Arturia Mini Brute Synthesizer, a Nord modular g2 and was recorded in Pro Tools.
